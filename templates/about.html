What is HireLens?

HireLens is a hiring intelligence platform purpose-built for the web3, crypto and tech markets. We continuously monitor Telegram channels, job boards, company career pages and aggregators — unifying everything into a single, AI-scored signal stream.

Every vacancy passes through our analysis pipeline: natural language understanding extracts structured data (role, seniority, stack, salary, location), while a 10-point scoring system evaluates quality, completeness and risk signals. Companies are automatically enriched with profiles, logos and market positioning.

Core Capabilities

Multi-Source Aggregation

Telegram channels, crypto job boards, career pages and ATS platforms — all unified into one stream with automatic deduplication.

🧠

AI Vacancy Scoring

Every vacancy receives a 10-point quality score based on description completeness, company legitimacy, salary transparency and risk signals.
